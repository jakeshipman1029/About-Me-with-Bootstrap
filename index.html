Pensacola Beach

Pensacola Beach with a jet of the Blue Angels

Pensacola, Florida, was my birthplace, however I do not remember a single thing about it before we moved to Alabama but on some occasions, my family drives down to Florida to meet some relatives and/or family friends. I love the beach and the landmarks such as the National Naval Aviation Museum. The resteraunts are also very good and give out a beach type of vibe.

From Florida, I went north up to North Alabama. I went to a public school that was pretty nice for me and not really for my brother. We decided the place called Grant, Alabama, isn't for us so we moved down from the mountains and moved to Huntsville where I would settle for about 4 years. 4 year later, my parents seperated and one went away to Tennessee(my mother) while the other one stayed in Huntsville(my dad). When we moved, we wanted to find a new school ASAP but they didn't want me to go to a public school. My neighbors recommended Jackson Christian and I was excited about it.
